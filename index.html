FAQs

Find answers to your most pressing questions about plant care, delivery, and returns.

How do I care?

Caring for your plants involves regular watering, ensuring they receive adequate sunlight, and occasional fertilization. Each plant has specific needs, so be sure to research the type you have. A little attention goes a long way in keeping them healthy.

What is delivery time?

Our standard delivery time is typically 5-7 business days. You will receive a tracking number once your order has shipped. For expedited options, please check our delivery page for details.

What is return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all plants. If your plant arrives damaged or unhealthy, please contact us for a replacement or refund. Ensure the plant is returned in its original condition.

Can I change order?

Yes, you can modify your order within 24 hours of placing it. Please contact our support team as soon as possible. After this period, we may not be able to accommodate changes.

Still need assistance?

Our customer service team is here to help you. Feel free to reach out with any questions or concerns. We strive to provide timely and helpful responses.
